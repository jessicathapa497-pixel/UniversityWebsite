Welcome to Nepal Public University

Nepal Public University is a pioneering institution in Nepal, dedicated to providing world-class education in Information Technology and E-Commerce. Established in 2010, we have been at the forefront of technological education, preparing students for the challenges of the digital age.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and practical learning has made us one of the most sought-after colleges for technology education in Nepal. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements.
