HISTORY

Our University

Founded in 1995 with a mission to advance the educational landscape of Nepal, Nepal Public University has grown into a premier public institution dedicated to academic excellence and social responsibility. For over three decades, we have remained committed to providing accessible, high-quality education that blends traditional values with modern research practices.

As a state-funded university, we serve as a vital hub for intellectual growth, fostering an environment where students from diverse backgrounds can excel in their chosen fields. Our legacy is built on a foundation of rigorous scholarship and a deep-seated commitment to the national development of Nepal, ensuring our graduates are prepared to lead both locally and globally.
